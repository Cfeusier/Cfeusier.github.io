Pair programming benefits

Driver

Clark's "Feelings"

I am much more comfortable driving, but need to avoid driving all the time.

James's "Feelings"

In the driver role I find it difficult to maintain my focus and keep myself from wanting to dive off into other areas of the code.

Navigator

Clark's "Feelings"

Don't second guess yourself when navigating, you are supposed to be directing someone.

James's "Feelings"

I generally feel more comfortable as the navigator if I feel we're on roughly the same level - this way I can just vocalize my thought process and lay out a high-level path for the driver.
